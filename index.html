About us

IUS Blue Wolves is a club that was founded from the love of basketball. Founder of the club is Mirnes Mesanovic, student of International University of Sarajevo. He realised potential of fellow students and immediately started getting everybody together. In short time team called Blue Wolves was created. The team has a strong desire to grow and develop one day in professional players.
